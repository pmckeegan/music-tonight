About

There is amazing live music happening all around us; we just need better ways to find it.

Launched in March of 2015, Music Tonight creates playlists of bands playing near you, wherever you are in the world. Partnering with Bandsintown, Spotify, and Rdio, Music Tonight makes playlists of nearby artists tonight, or up two weeks in the future.

You won't like most of the songs on the playlist we make for you. You probably won't like one track out of ten. But, equiped with a skip button and a sense of adventure, we hope you love it half as much as we do.

Go out.
